About Anaam Group

Since 1995, Anaam International Holding Group has been at the forefront of Saudi Arabia's economic transformation. With a market capitalization of 680 million SAR, we continue to drive innovation, create value, and contribute to the realization of Saudi Vision 2030.

Our commitment to excellence and strategic investments across key sectors have positioned us as a leading force in shaping the Kingdom's future. As a publicly traded company (Symbol: 4061) on the Saudi Stock Exchange, we are dedicated to delivering value to our shareholders and stakeholders alike.
